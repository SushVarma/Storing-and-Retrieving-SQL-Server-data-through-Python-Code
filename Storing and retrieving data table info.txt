Table description:
 
1. Admin table:
     This table gives information about the administrator of a particular corporation (ex: JinHit Enterprises).
2. Dev table:
     This table gives information about various devices managed by the admin and distributed to employees of a particular corporation.
3. Own table
      This table gives information about the employees of a particular corporation who are in charge of any devices in the office provided by the administrator .
4.Rental Table
      This table gives information about the people(registered)  who are in charge of any devices provided by the administrator on a rent basis.
5. Home table
      This table gives information about the of a particular corporation who are in charge of any devices at home provided by the administrator.
6. Supp table
      This table gives information about the employees of a particular corporation who are in charge of  supplying devices to the administrator.
